TRUCKIN' FIT 

TruckinFit is a brand that offers a specially curated kit designed for truckers to stay healthy and clean on the road. Truck drivers often spend long hours on the road, making it difficult for them to find clean and accessible hygiene facilities. We believe in making a positive impact on the health and well-being, and therefore also safety of truckers everywhere. Our products are of the highest quality and meet the unique needs of truckers. Let us help you live your best life on the road. Stay #TruckinFit.

We offer a "Health Kit" specifically designed for truckers. This kit consists of seven products that will help you stay healthy and fit on the road!

OUR KIT 

Vitamin pills: Vitamin pills are an essential part of the kit for truck drivers as they can help support a healthy immune system. Long hours behind the wheel and irregular eating habits can reduce the immune function of truck drivers. Vitamin pills, such as multivitamins or vitamin C supplements, can help promote the overall health and resistance of truck drivers.


Portable shower bag: A portable shower bag is a convenient tool for truck drivers to freshen up during long trips. It can be difficult to have access to a shower on the go, especially during long-distance drives. A portable shower bag allows you to shower quickly and easily without relying on external facilities, which can help maintain the hygiene of truck drivers.

Wet wipes: Wet wipes are handy for truck drivers to freshen up and stay clean between stops. They are especially useful for quickly cleaning hands and face, especially when there is no access to water and soap. Wet wipes can help reduce bacteria and germs and improve the personal hygiene of truck drivers.


6kg weights: 6kg weights are a great addition to the kit to promote the fitness of truck drivers. Prolonged sitting and lack of exercise can affect the physical health of truck drivers. By carrying 6kg weights, truck drivers can do simple strength training exercises during stops to strengthen muscles and stay fit.

Hand sanitizer: Hand sanitizer is an important product in the kit to ensure the hygiene of truck drivers, especially in the current times of pandemics and infectious diseases. Regularly cleaning hands and surfaces can help reduce the spread of germs and protect the health of truck drivers.

Resistance bands: Resistance bands are a convenient fitness tool for truck drivers to maintain muscle strength and flexibility. They are compact and portable, allowing truck drivers to easily carry them in the kit. With resistance bands, drivers can do various exercises to train different muscle groups, even in limited spaces such as the cabin of a truck.

OUR VISION

At TruckinFit, our vision is to create a culture of health and wellness among truckers, where they can prioritize their physical and mental health, and ultimately improve their safety on the road. We aim to empower truckers to take control of their health and well-being, by offering innovative solutions that are easy to use and accessible.We understand the unique challenges faced by truckers who spend long hours on the road and the difficulty they face in maintaining their hygiene and health. Our goal is to address these challenges by providing high-quality products that are specially curated to meet the needs of truckers.
