MANAGEMENT & LEAN CANVAS 

Interviews

Assumption 1: In our first round of interviews, we noticed people had a hard time moving. In our second interview, we re-asked this, and they all said that it was hard because you don’t have the equipment to move. So in our box for truckers, we want to include at least 2 tools that can help to move a trucker when he has a break.

Assumption 2: In the first interviews we spoke to men and women but we didn’t really see any differences in the problems they had. In the second round of interviews, there was a call with a woman in the truck driving industry. And she told me that they experience more hygiene problems then men do. That’s why we introduced a personalized box so women that need more hygiene products can choose from this. 


Pricing 

In Our solution interviews we came to the conclusion that there should be a standard (normal) box to choose from. But if you already have the standard box or want to change a few items you can use 12 items and personalize your own box. In the interviews, people told us that they would like to have items that they can use for long term, they where willing to pay between 25 and 40 euro’s for a normal packages.

MARKETING

What is our brand about?

TruckinFit is a brand that offers a specially curated kit designed for truckers to stay 
healthy and clean on the road. Truck drivers often spend long hours on the road, 
making it difficult to find clean and accessible hygiene facilities. We believe in 
positively impacting the health and well-being, and therefore also the safety of 
truckers everywhere. Our products are of the highest quality and meet the unique needs 
of truckers. Let us help you live your best life on the road. Stay #TruckinFit.   

Media channels truckers use:


Radio:
While truck drivers are on the road mainly use a transmitter - Sirius XM Radio – radio10. They will use this to entertain themselves when driving for long hours. Gerard Ekdom is the favorite radio DJ among Dutch truck drivers (Olsthoorn, 2019). 

Facebook:

According to a survey by Trucker News, 63% of surveyed drivers said they use Facebook, making it the leading social media site of choice for truckers. In addition, 54% said they use YouTube. The percentage of drivers on Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, and Pinterest ranged from 11% to 15%.
When asked, “On a scale of 0 to 10 (where 0 = never and 10 = constantly), how frequently do you use these online network resources to get information about the trucking industry?” Facebook finished third. LinkedIn earned a rating of 6, Instagram was rated 5.1, and Facebook 5. (Hollis, 2019) 

Digital outdoor media:

Billboards along the road. While driving on the highway, your attention is drawn to large digital screens with a clear message. This practical way of advertising is common throughout the Netherlands. You can advertise effectively not only along the major highways of our country but also along other busy roads. It's an easy way to reach many people in a short time. This form of advertising cannot be blocked. When motorists take the same route more often, they will remember your company better. (Reclame Langs De Weg - Bereik, 2021) 

Clarification:

Because of the significant differences, we saw how many truckdrivers did use Facebook and also to be realistic to reach our objective. We had chosen to only go for Facebook. Radio would also be a good option, but it is outside our budget for this project and makes reaching the designated target group harder. We also saw that truckers are part of really big Facebook groups that is really popular right now. So if we can, we would use one of those groups to post in there.  We didn’t manage to get or objective yet. Also because we had said it on 28 of may. What means that if we would have done some adjustments we would probably make it to or objective.  

Also, we noticed throughout posting that posting in Dutch worked better than in English. Besides the target group where of international drivers, we did want to focus on Dutch-speaking people. And Dutch truck drivers prefer to get their content within social media in their preferred language.  

   

Statistics showed that if we liked more of other accounts and contributed to another account on the platform, it also helped us reach more. So a learning point can be being consistent yourself but also trying to engage with the content of others so they can find you as well.

In this post, we could see in the statistics that videos did better than just images. And a learning point for us is that we needed to post more videos. So in the future, we want to focus more on putting reels/video’s out on the platform. We did post reel videos and also saw that our account got more reach because of that, even a person that wasn’t following or Facebook account.

FUTURE PLANNING 

For the future we have decided to increase our content output on social media. Within our posts so far we realised that video posts (reels) perform on average much better than pictures. We have created another 4 reels to share over the coming week. Furthermore we want to limit the language to Dutch, as those posts got more engagement. We also want to interact more with users on our site (commenting, following, liking) as we already receive inquiries from users about where to find our products. 

TESTING REPORT 

Our testing goals
We want to know if our website is clear and if the user understands the purpose of our brand.
Participant
We want to aim for participants both in the trucking industry, aswell as the creative industry
Setting
We would prefer a user testing in person as it allows for more information, if that is not available online is an option as well
Protocol
We want users to navigate around the website to.
1. Find our product and relevant info
2. Share their opinion on our product, potential shortcoming and the design
3. Navigate to the page about our team and company 
4. Find the Help section with the FAQ 
5. Find the way to contact us for more information (find hyperlink to facebook) 
6. Provide feedback on the overall design of our website, with regards to their personal feelings as well as the target audience

JUSTIFICATION 


Website
For the website we chose to go with a rather simple theme that make it easy to navigate. However we still wanted to separate it in pages for easier navigation, as we felt having everything on one page was too much. The website follows our color scheme to create a united underlaying design language for all our content spanning from our website, our product mockup to our facebook page and posts. As our basic template we used a template from mobirise (https://mobirise.com/extensions/softwarem4/web-saas-app/)  as it was sugested by a fellow classmate. After downloading the code we altered it to fit within our needs. For extra features like the video banner on the main page we used tutorials aswell as code from W3 School.    

Product Demo 
When we decided to come up with a product/demo at the beginning of the project, we said we wanted to have three boxes. But because we are a start-up company, having so many options wouldn’t be realistic, so we decided to go with just one package. Then we finished or solution interviews, and people liked the chance to have a standard box but also wanted to have the opportunity to personalize the box. So that’s a big difference we change through speaking with our customers. Or strategy was to make a small box with a handle so truck drivers could bring it with them, and it would also fit in the small cabin of the truck drivers. All the products in the box can be used in or around the truck, making it practical for them. We also look at what an example person could be for truckers so they can see what they can become.
