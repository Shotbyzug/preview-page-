What is our brand about?

TruckinFit is a brand that offers a specially curated kit designed for truckers to stay 
healthy and clean on the road. Truck drivers often spend long hours on the road, 
making it difficult to find clean and accessible hygiene facilities. We believe in 
positively impacting the health and well-being, and therefore also the safety of 
truckers everywhere. Our products are of the highest quality and meet the unique needs 
of truckers. Let us help you live your best life on the road. Stay #TruckinFit.   

Media channels truckers use:


Radio:
While truck drivers are on the road mainly use a transmitter - Sirius XM Radio – radio10. They will use this to entertain themselves when driving for long hours. Gerard Ekdom is the favorite radio DJ among Dutch truck drivers (Olsthoorn, 2019). 

Facebook:

According to a survey by Trucker News, 63% of surveyed drivers said they use Facebook, making it the leading social media site of choice for truckers. In addition, 54% said they use YouTube. The percentage of drivers on Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, and Pinterest ranged from 11% to 15%.
When asked, “On a scale of 0 to 10 (where 0 = never and 10 = constantly), how frequently do you use these online network resources to get information about the trucking industry?” Facebook finished third. LinkedIn earned a rating of 6, Instagram was rated 5.1, and Facebook 5. (Hollis, 2019) 

Digital outdoor media:

Billboards along the road. While driving on the highway, your attention is drawn to large digital screens with a clear message. This practical way of advertising is common throughout the Netherlands. You can advertise effectively not only along the major highways of our country but also along other busy roads. It's an easy way to reach many people in a short time. This form of advertising cannot be blocked. When motorists take the same route more often, they will remember your company better. (Reclame Langs De Weg - Bereik, 2021) 

Clarification:

Because of the significant differences, we saw how many truckdrivers did use Facebook and also to be realistic to reach our objective. We had chosen to only go for Facebook. Radio would also be a good option, but it is outside our budget for this project and makes reaching the designated target group harder. We also saw that truckers are part of really big Facebook groups that is really popular right now. So if we can, we would use one of those groups to post in there.  We didn’t manage to get or objective yet. Also because we had said it on 28 of may. What means that if we would have done some adjustments we would probably make it to or objective.  

Also, we noticed throughout posting that posting in Dutch worked better than in English. Besides the target group where of international drivers, we did want to focus on Dutch-speaking people. And Dutch truck drivers prefer to get their content within social media in their preferred language.  

   

Statistics showed that if we liked more of other accounts and contributed to another account on the platform, it also helped us reach more. So a learning point can be being consistent yourself but also trying to engage with the content of others so they can find you as well.

In this post, we could see in the statistics that videos did better than just images. And a learning point for us is that we needed to post more videos. So in the future, we want to focus more on putting reels/video’s out on the platform. We did post reel videos and also saw that our account got more reach because of that, even a person that wasn’t following or Facebook account.

Website
For the website we chose to go with a rather simple theme that make it easy to navigate. However we still wanted to separate it in pages for easier navigation, as we felt having everything on one page was too much. The website follows our color scheme to create a united underlaying design language for all our content spanning from our website, our product mockup to our facebook page and posts. As our basic template we used a template from mobirise (https://mobirise.com/extensions/softwarem4/web-saas-app/)  as it was sugested by a fellow classmate. After downloading the code we altered it to fit within our needs. For extra features like the video banner on the main page we used tutorials aswell as conde from W3 School.
